## The Question Everyone Asks

"Should I use ChatGPT or Claude?" If you're an Etsy seller trying to integrate AI into your workflow, this is probably the first decision you face. And the honest answer is: it depends on what you're doing.

I've used both extensively across every aspect of running an Etsy shop. Here's what I've found after six months of real-world testing.

## Product Descriptions

**ChatGPT** writes product descriptions that are energetic, punchy, and sales-forward. It's naturally inclined toward marketing copy. Give it your product details and it'll produce something that reads like a professional copywriter wrote it.

**Claude** writes descriptions that are more measured and detailed. It tends to be more accurate to the source material and less likely to embellish or make claims you didn't provide. If your product is handmade with specific materials, Claude will respect those details.

**Winner**: ChatGPT for emotional appeal, Claude for accuracy. Use ChatGPT for lifestyle products where the vibe matters. Use Claude for technical or handmade items where precision builds trust.

## Customer Service Drafts

This is where the difference is stark.

**ChatGPT** generates customer service responses that are professional but can feel templated. It defaults to corporate-friendly language that might not match your brand voice.

**Claude** is significantly better at maintaining a consistent, warm tone across messages. It's also better at handling nuanced situations — a customer who's upset but has a valid complaint, or a polite request that you technically can't fulfill.

**Winner**: Claude, clearly. Its responses feel more human and more attuned to the emotional context of the conversation.

## SEO and Keyword Research

**ChatGPT** with browsing enabled can pull in current search data and suggest keywords based on real trends. With plugins, it can connect to tools like Marmalead or eRank data.

**Claude** can work with keyword data you provide but doesn't have real-time search access in the same way. However, it's better at organizing and prioritizing keywords into a coherent strategy.

**Winner**: ChatGPT for research and discovery, Claude for strategy and organization. Ideally, use both in sequence.

## Bulk Operations

If you need to write 50 product titles, 30 tag sets, or rewrite an entire shop's worth of descriptions, **ChatGPT** handles volume better. It's faster and maintains more consistency across large batches.

**Claude** is better for careful, one-at-a-time work where each piece needs to be thoughtfully crafted.

**Winner**: ChatGPT for volume, Claude for quality.

## The Real Answer

The best approach is to use both. ChatGPT as your first-draft machine and brainstorming partner. Claude as your editor, strategist, and customer-facing voice. They cost roughly the same, and the combined workflow produces results that neither can match alone.

Stop picking sides. Start building a system.
